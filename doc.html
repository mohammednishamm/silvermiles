DR. MEAGAN STURM Orthodontist

Dr. Meagan Sturm, Orthodontist

Dr. Meagan Sturm was raised in Marietta, Georgia. Dr. Meagan attended Emory University, where she graduated cum laude with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Psychology. She went on to dental school at Medical College of Georgia where she found dentistry to be a perfect mix of her love of art and science. Dr. Meagan’s biggest source of satisfaction is watching the transformation of each of her patients from their first day of treatment until their perfect smile is achieved. She prides herself on the quality of the work she provides and strives to do her best to provide each patient with the highest quality of care.
